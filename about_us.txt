English

    We design and transform green spaces with creativity, purpose, and experience.

    We are a multidisciplinary team passionate about nature, composed of a visual artist specialized in green projects, an outdoor space construction professional, experienced gardeners, and a specialist in native plants and cultivation.

    We offer comprehensive gardening and landscaping services:

    Garden design and creation: from ornamental, romantic, and creative styles to sustainable, budget-friendly, or avant-garde gardens.

    Ponds and fountains: we create aquatic features that bring beauty and harmony to your space.

    Garden infrastructure: we build paths, walls, stairs, benches, and structures using stone, wood, and various materials.

    Irrigation systems: we install underground irrigation and rainwater harvesting systems.

    Green walls and meditation corners with kokedamas and live plants, ideal for interiors, balconies, or compact spaces.

    Space transformation: we can turn your balcony, terrace, or even your garage into a green oasis.

    Urban farming: we design and build raised beds, chicken coops, and composting systems for a self-sustainable lifestyle.


    Also available:

    Private or group botanical art classes (Spanish only).

    Natural souvenirs and centerpieces for weddings, baptisms, quinceañeras, and special events.
