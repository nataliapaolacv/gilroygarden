About Us

We are a multidisciplinary team passionate about nature, composed of a visual artist specialized in green projects, an outdoor space construction professional, experienced gardeners, and a specialist in native plants and cultivation.

Our Services

Garden design and creation

From ornamental, romantic, and creative styles to sustainable, budget-friendly, or avant-garde gardens.

Ponds and fountains

We create aquatic features that bring beauty and harmony to your space.

Garden infrastructure

We build paths, walls, stairs, benches, and structures using stone, wood, and various materials.

Irrigation systems

We install underground irrigation and rainwater harvesting systems.

Green walls and meditation corners

We create green walls and meditation corners with kokedamas and live plants, ideal for interiors, balconies, or compact spaces.

Space transformation

We can turn your balcony, terrace, or even your garage into a green oasis.

Urban farming

We design and build raised beds, chicken coops, and composting systems for a self-sustainable lifestyle.
